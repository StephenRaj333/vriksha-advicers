Vriksha Advisors

Boutique Investment advisors for founders and investors in India

Vriksha Advisors is a boutique Investment Advisory firm specialising in working with early and mid stage startups. We offer a bouquet of services including fund raising, legal & compliance, accounts outsourcing and business plan preparation.

We have a strong network built with HNIs, Family Offices, Venture Capital & Private Equity Firms and Institutional Investors. Our experienced team has worked with a number of startups on fund-raising, secondary exits, M&A, consulting and due-diligence.

We have a team of experienced experts who have worked in the financial services and banking industry, with 40+ years of combined experience. We are partnered with top legal, valuation and accounting firms as well to provide end-to-end coverage in case of more complex transactions.
